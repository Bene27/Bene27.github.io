Design Principles

Repetiton

Instagram


Instagram's Website

The instagram offrical website shows proper repetition of content and element.There is a cohesive relationship between the those elements.

The website repeats a new feater, an example of the new feature and a summary explanning the new feature. This repetition continues until the end of the home page.

Contrast

The Church Of Jesus Christ

The Church of Jesus Christ's Website

In order to enhance readability, there is a contrast of colors between the pink and black text.

There is also a contrast in the font size of the headings and the paragraph. This is so helpful for the reader.This effect also makes the whole page visually appealing.

Alignment

Rain

Rain's Website

The elements on the page are effectively aligned with one another.This effect improves the overall experience for the user.

The writings , as well as the images are all centered on the middle of the page.
