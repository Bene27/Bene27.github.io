Target Audience:

  1. Any Gender.

Two Personas:

A Business man named Ramsey Martin

  • Age- 35 years old
  • Works at a law firm
  • He works long hours at work, and he likes to be comfortable during his long hours at work.
  • He likes going for runs after work and he likes knowing beforehand if it will rain , or rain

A female travel guide named Abigel Smith

  • Age- 25 years old
  • Is outside for most of her day
  • Since she is always outside, she needs to know accurate weather predictions.
  • She leaves her house at 6:00am , and needs to know the daily weather the night before.
